What is Gremlin

"Gremlin is the graph traversal language of Apache TinkerPop. Gremlin is a functional, data-flow language that enables users to succinctly express complex traversals on (or queries of) their application's property graph...."

-https://tinkerpop.apache.org/gremlin.html

Why GQB?

I built this site as another way to follow along with the Kevin Lawrence's Practical Gremlin. It has been one of the most helpful resources on getting started with Gremlin. This project is intented to be used as a brief introduction to Gremlin before going into the full details and deep dive into the documentation itself.
